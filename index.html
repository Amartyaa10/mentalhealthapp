WHO-Recommended Relaxation & Coping Resources

Curated from the World Health Organization's guidelines for adolescent mental health, these strategies focus on building resilience against stress and anxiety.

Core Coping Strategies
  • Healthy Sleep Patterns: Aim for 8-10 hours nightly to regulate mood and cognition.
  • Regular Exercise: 30 minutes daily, like walking or yoga, to reduce anxiety.
  • Skill-Building: Develop problem-solving, emotional management, and interpersonal skills through journaling or apps.
  • Alternatives to Risk-Taking: Replace unhelpful habits (e.g., substance use) with positive outlets like creative hobbies.
Prevention & Interventions
  • Promote socio-emotional learning in schools to foster supportive environments.
  • Use digital platforms for accessible mental health education.
  • Access WHO's mhGAP Intervention Guide for evidence-based protocols: Download Guide
  • Join the Helping Adolescents Thrive (HAT) Initiative: Learn More
Source: WHO Fact Sheet on Adolescent Mental Health. For immediate support, contact Tele-MANAS (India): 14416.
